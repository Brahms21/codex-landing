CodeX is a group of software engineers looking to collaborate, learn and solve real world problems. We strive on the philosophy that each member's ideas will be heard and taken into consideration, and upon depolyment our projects will profit each member equally. As CodeX grows and members elevate their abilities, we will continue to push for larger projects and continue to solve larger problems. Solutions for X, as a variable X can mean many things, use any problem in your life as X. Through engineering we believe we can solve for X together.

With four front-end developers able to handle design and UI, we are looking for back-end developers wanting to level up their skills. Node and Python are the preferred server side languages. We also need people to create and manage databases. This group is for you if want to work as a team and get away from solo developing. See what your small ideas can blossom into here. The only thing we require is time and effort.
